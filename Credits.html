Credits and Attributions

“Desi Short” is powered by a collaborative network of technologies and third-party services. We gratefully acknowledge the contributions of the following:

1. Content Aggregation and Processing

  • News Sources:

    • The News API and News Data IO provide the raw news content aggregated from multiple reputable sources.

  • Content Summarization:

    • AI technologies, including models similar to GPT, along with human editorial oversight, are used to generate concise 60-word summaries.

  • Translation Services:

    • Translations into nine local languages are powered by Google Firebase ML Kit.

2. Multimedia and Interactive Features

  • Text-to-Speech and Audio:

    • Our in-app technologies convert text summaries into audio for an accessible user experience.

  • Video Content:

    • Short news videos, created by AI or human editors, are hosted and streamed via YouTube.

  • Social and Interactive Features:

    • Features such as comments, sharing, and polls enhance user engagement.

3. Advertising and Revenue

  • Ad Platforms:

    • Advertising revenue is generated using Google AdMob and Facebook Ads.

  • Subscription Services:

    • Additional premium content may be accessed through subscription plans.

4. Design Assets and External Resources

  • Icons and Graphical Elements:

    • Visual assets, including icons sourced from Flaticon, Icon8, and similar platforms, enhance the app’s interface. we will remove your icons if it not satisfied with your policy. please contact us.

  • Third-Party Libraries:

    • Various software libraries and tools are used under open-source licenses to build and maintain the app.

5. Acknowledgments

We extend our sincere thanks to all service providers, developers, designers, and partners who help make “Desi Short” an engaging and innovative news platform. Their expertise and commitment enable us to deliver high-quality, localized news in a user-friendly format.
