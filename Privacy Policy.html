Privacy Policy

“Desi Short” ("we", "us", or "our") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your personal data when you use our mobile application, “Desi Short,” which offers 60-word news summaries in nine local languages, with text-to-speech, multimedia features, social sharing, and additional services like insights, quotes, and polls. We also integrate advertising platforms such as Google AdMob, Facebook Ads, and may offer subscription-based services as revenue channels.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account, subscribe, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Identification Data: Your name, username, email address, and phone number.

  • Account Credentials: Passwords or other security information.

  • Profile Data: Any additional details you provide for personalization.

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your interactions with the app, including:

  • Interaction Data: Pages viewed, news summaries accessed, time spent on the app, and features used (e.g., text-to-speech, comments, shares, filters).

  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, and mobile network information.

  • Analytics Data: Information generated from cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to analyze usage trends and improve our services.

2.3 Third-Party Data

We obtain data from third-party providers, including:

  • News Content Sources: Data aggregated from The News API, News Data IO, and other reputable news providers.

  • Translation Services: Data processed via Google Firebase ML Kit.

  • Advertising and Analytics: Data provided by Google AdMob, Facebook Ads, and other advertising networks to personalize and optimize ads.

  • Social and Multimedia Platforms: Data from services like YouTube when you view short news videos.

2.4 How We Collect Data

Data is collected through:

  • Direct Interactions: When you fill out forms, subscribe, or contact us.

  • Automated Technologies: Through cookies, log files, and similar technologies that track your activity.

  • Third-Party Integrations: When you access content from integrated services or when third-party partners provide data to enhance our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for various purposes:

  • Providing Services: To deliver localized, short news summaries and multimedia content (e.g., text-to-speech audio, videos).

  • Personalization: To tailor content to your language and regional preferences.

  • Communication: To send you updates, newsletters, alerts, or marketing communications (with your consent, where required by law).

  • Analytics and Improvement: To analyze user behavior and improve our app’s functionality, usability, and content offerings.

  • Monetization: To display ads through Google AdMob, Facebook Ads, or to manage subscription-based services.

  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, and protect our rights and those of our users.
